01. Last Flamenco

2:34

A daring statement of self-expression blending Flamenco rhythms with custom electronic textures. Demonstrates a novel multilayered stem mastering technique that achieves extreme loudness (-4 LUFS) while preserving dynamic clarity—proving that unconventional methods can yield professional fidelity.

02. Star Atlas

3:12

Composed a percussion- and electronics-based score for the Morehead Planetarium. Collaborated with Prof. Lee Weisert. The piece serves as a prologue, intended to illuminate the mysterious beauty of the universe while also exploring the overwhelming terror and loneliness in space.
